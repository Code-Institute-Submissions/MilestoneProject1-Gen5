Xbox Series X

Microsoft have doubled down on their strengths with the release of the xbox series x. The processing power of this machine is staggering, moving beyond a personal gaming system and rivaling personal computers. This should give the series x unprecidented speeds and make "load up times" a thing of the past. With new frachise announcements due and old favourites making a return, amazing experiences are on the horizon.

Accessories

Xbox Wireless Controller

Built for comfort and performance, this new ergonomic design makes the latest xbox controler their most comfortable to use, ever. New features incluse a dedicated share button to make streaming a simple process. Available on Game.com, retail price £59.99.

Xbox Gaming Headphones

With the series x bringing innovations in suroundsound and 3D tracking, its more important than ever to find yourself a good set of headphones. This one change can highten the experience and bring emersion to an all time high. Available on Game.com, average retail price £24.99 - £49.99.

Previous Console Accessories

Mcrosoft has made it a priority to make their technology reverse compatible, but this extends to more than the games. Accessories of previous consoles (e.g.the xbox kinect) will work with the latest system and games. Available on Game.com, retail price £49.99.
